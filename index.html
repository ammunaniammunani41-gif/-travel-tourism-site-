Explore Kerala

Kerala, "God's Own Country", is famous for its backwaters, beaches, and hill stations. Kochi is a vibrant coastal city blending culture, history, and modern attractions.

Kochi Highlights

  • Fort Kochi Heritage Streets
  • Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Marine Drive Walk
  • Mattancherry & Jew Town
  • Lulu Mall Shopping

Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is the heart of Ooty, surrounded by misty hills and eucalyptus trees. Visitors enjoy boating, cycling, and photography.

Things to Do

  • Pedal, row & motor boat rides
  • Lakeside cycling
  • Photography with mountain views
  • Mini amusement rides for kids
